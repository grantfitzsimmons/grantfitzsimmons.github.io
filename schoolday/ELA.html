English Language Arts 11

In ELA 11 I read multiple books throughout the year in a cycle and analyze each for the content that is not directly stated in the story. The novels we read as a class and analyze help prepare me for college and enhance my understanding of literary devices and the choices many authors make to communicate a message to their audience.
