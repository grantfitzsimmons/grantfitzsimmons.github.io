AP US History

AP US History explores the history of America from pre-European colonization briefly to the much more important British invasion and conquering. We study the expansion of the original 13 colonies, the spread west and manifest destiny, and the growth and fall of the American monopoly pioneers. This class is designed to educate any student on how the America we live in today came to be and how the actions in the past shape the modern day.
