Symphonic Band

In Symphonic Band, my class practices pre-arranged music to become prepared for a concert every two months or so. We learn and are graded on pitch accuracy, rhythm, tempo, articulation, musicality, and tone. Scales and warm ups are a large portion of the day to widen our range and articulation strength.
