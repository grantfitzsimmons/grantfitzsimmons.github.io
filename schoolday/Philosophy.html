Philosophy

Philosophy calls into question every fundamental part of life and asks the question "Why?". The class is meant to cause self-reflection and questions the traditional beliefs of the world and how they came to be. We learn the history of Socrates, Plato, and Athens as well as where and how many of the traditional philosophical beliefs came to be.
