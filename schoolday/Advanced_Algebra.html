Advanced Algebra

Advanced Algebra is a college-readiness/college credit class. It is similar to Algebra 2, however the course is rigorous and never stops to breath. There is not a day to review or take time to study an old topic as it is a constant stream of new information and notes. I would not recommend this course for the faint of heart, as it can be extremely stressful, however it is regarded as an easy math class for anyone who has a lot of experience in that field. I, however, am not as proficient at math as in many other subjects.
