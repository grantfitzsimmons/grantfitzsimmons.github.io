Chemistry

In Chemistry, we learn how to balance equations, understand why chemical reactions happen the way they do, and memorize many equations and facts about chemistry. This class has homework every night and a rigorous workload in school as well. Luckily, there are many opportunities to review and ask for help during husky half time and throughout lab and lesson time. Mrs. Kennedy, my chemistry teacher, does a fantastic job of reaching out and helping students when they do not understand a topic.
