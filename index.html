3 Places I Would Like to Travel

Berlin, Germany

Germany is a diverse nation with a long and rich history. I chose to study and research it as it has one of the most interesting population makeups, large history of warfare in a global sense, and radical shifts in political power and beliefs over the years.

Oslo, Norway

I chose to study Oslo, Norway as it is the capital of one of the most influential cities worldwide and the de facto hub of Norwegian trade, baking, shipping, and industry. It has an incredibly high standard of living and is a model for other European/Scandinavian cities to build off of.

Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo, Japan is an incredible example of a long time culturally-isolated pacific power shaped itself into a worldwide trading center and prosperous metropolitan area. Its home to major corporations and huge manufacturing giants, as well as many small businesses and communities.
